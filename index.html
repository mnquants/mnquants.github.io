Student organization focused on quantitative research and financial engineering

Projects

We mainly focus on research projects. Our projects have ranged from researching trading strategies to pricing exotic derivatives. Click here for our project gallery. If you are a student with a project idea, we encourage you to apply.

Experiences

We gain as much exposure to the quantitative finance industry as we can through trading competitions and talks with industry professionals.

Collaboration

We pool our knowledge and resources to navigate this competitive industry. We are fortunate to have active alumni and industry experts who advise our efforts.

Join us

We look for STEM students with a demonstrated interest in financial markets. Programming experience, willingness to learn, and productivity are important for your success in this club.
